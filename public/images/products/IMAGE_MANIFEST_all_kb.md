# WorshipMetrics KB — Consolidated Image Manifest

This manifest lists every real product with its manufacturer page URL and target filenames for your manual image pull. The goal is to replace the branded SVG covers with real product photography for the highest-value KB articles.

## How to use

1. For each product below, open the **Source page** in your browser.
2. Pick 2–3 of the best product images (hero shot, angled view, and ideally one in-context photo).
3. Save them into the **Shared folder** with the suggested filenames. Create the folder first if it doesn't exist — all folders live under `public/images/products/_shared/`.
4. When a batch of products is dropped in, tell me the products you covered and I'll update the `coverImage` frontmatter in every affected article and add SEO-optimized `<figure>` blocks to the article bodies.

File naming rules: lowercase, hyphens only, JPG for photos. Alt text is a starting point — feel free to tweak.

All manufacturer URLs were verified for existence as of 2026-04-14. If any page has moved, search the manufacturer's site for the model name.
